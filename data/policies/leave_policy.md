# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month from the start date.
- Leave must be requested at least 5 business days in advance for periods of 3+ days.
- Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next year.
- Leave requests are subject to manager approval based on business needs.

## Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- A medical certificate is required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days.
- Sick leave does not carry over to the following year.
- Employees should notify their manager as early as possible on the day of absence.

## Personal Leave
- 5 days of personal leave are available per year for personal matters.
- Personal leave must be approved by the direct manager.
- Unused personal leave does not carry over.

## Parental Leave
- Primary caregivers: 16 weeks of paid parental leave.
- Secondary caregivers: 4 weeks of paid parental leave.
- Leave can be taken within 12 months of the birth/adoption.
- Employees must notify HR at least 8 weeks before the expected start date.

## Bereavement Leave
- 5 days for immediate family members (spouse, children, parents, siblings).
- 3 days for extended family members.

## Public Holidays
- All employees are entitled to national public holidays as per their work location.
- If required to work on a public holiday, employees receive time-off-in-lieu.

## Leave Request Process
1. Submit request through the HR platform
2. Manager reviews and approves/denies
3. HR records the leave
4. Payroll adjusted accordingly
