# Employee Benefits Policy

## Health Insurance
- Company provides comprehensive health insurance for all full-time employees.
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment.
- Plans available: Basic, Standard, and Premium.
- The company covers 80% of the premium; employees cover 20%.
- Dependents can be added at additional cost.
- Annual open enrollment period is November 1-30.

## Dental Insurance
- Standard and Premium dental plans available.
- Coverage includes preventive care (2 cleanings/year), basic procedures, and major procedures.
- Company covers 70% of the dental premium.

## Vision Insurance
- Annual eye exam covered at 100%.
- Frames and lenses allowance of $200 per year.
- Contact lens allowance of $150 per year.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Eligible from day one of employment.
- Company matches employee contributions up to 4% of salary.
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years.
- Employees can contribute up to the IRS annual limit.

## Life Insurance
- Company provides basic life insurance at 2x annual salary at no cost to employees.
- Supplemental life insurance available for purchase up to 5x salary.
- AD&D (Accidental Death & Dismemberment) coverage included.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Free, confidential counseling and support services.
- Available 24/7 via phone: 1-800-EAP-HELP.
- Covers: mental health, financial counseling, legal assistance, substance abuse support.
- Up to 8 free counseling sessions per year per issue.

## Professional Development
- Annual learning budget of $2,000 per employee.
- Conference attendance allowance (1-2 per year with manager approval).
- Internal training programs and mentorship available.

## Remote Work
- Hybrid work model: minimum 2 days in office per week.
- Full remote positions available based on role and manager approval.
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time setup + $50/month for internet.

## Wellness Programs
- $500 annual wellness reimbursement (gym, fitness, mental health apps).
- Monthly wellness activities and workshops.
- Ergonomic assessment for workstation setup.
