# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful workplace for all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Expected Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with dignity, respect, and fairness.
- Foster an inclusive environment that values diverse perspectives.
- Communicate professionally and constructively.
- Respect confidentiality of company and personal information.
- Follow all company policies and applicable laws.

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:
- Harassment of any kind (verbal, physical, visual, or online).
- Discrimination based on race, gender, age, disability, religion, sexual orientation, or any protected characteristic.
- Bullying, intimidation, or retaliation.
- Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information.
- Misuse of company resources or property.
- Substance abuse in the workplace.
- Conflicts of interest without disclosure.

## Reporting Concerns
- Employees should report concerns to their manager, HR, or via the confidential hotline.
- All reports will be investigated promptly and thoroughly.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.
- Anonymous reporting is available through the HR platform.

## Disciplinary Action
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, depending on the severity of the offense.

## Anti-Harassment Policy
- Sexual harassment, unwelcome advances, and hostile work environment behaviors are prohibited.
- This applies to all company spaces, events, and online communications.
- Managers have a duty to report any harassment they observe or learn about.

## Conflict of Interest
- Employees must disclose any situation that may create a conflict of interest.
- Outside employment must not interfere with company responsibilities.
- Gifts from vendors/clients exceeding $50 in value must be reported.

## Data Privacy
- Handle personal data according to company privacy policies and applicable laws.
- Only access data you need for your job responsibilities.
- Report any suspected data breaches to IT Security immediately.
